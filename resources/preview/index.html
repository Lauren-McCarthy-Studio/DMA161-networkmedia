DESMA161: Network Media

PREVIEWING MOBILE SITES IN CHROME

  1. Right click anywhere on your page and click "Select".
  2. You should see a panel open on the bottom or side of your window. Click the mobile icon.
  3. Click the dropdown next to the device name in the top of the window to select other sizes.
